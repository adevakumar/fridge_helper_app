Banana Bread

Difficulty: Easy Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 55 minutes Yield: Makes one loaf

Moist and delicious banana bread recipe. Easy to make, no need for a mixer! Ripe bananas, butter, sugar, egg, vanilla, baking soda, and flour.

INGREDIENTS 2 to 3 very ripe bananas, peeled 1/3 cup melted butter 1 teaspoon baking soda Pinch of salt 3/4 cup sugar 1 large egg, beaten 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1.5 cups of all-purpose flour

Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350째F (175째C), or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean. 5 Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving. Slice and serve. (A bread knife helps to make slices that aren't crumbly.)
